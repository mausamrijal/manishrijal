About

Hello, I'm Manish, a passionate and dedicated cook with a flair for creating memorable culinary experiences. With a year of experience at the renowned 5-star Hyatt Kathmandu, I've honed my skills and developed a deep appreciation for the art of cooking. My love for the culinary arts began as a mere fascination, but it quickly blossomed into a full-fledged career. After obtaining my diploma, I embarked on a journey that took me to the heart of the kitchen at Hyatt Kathmandu, where I had the privilege of working with some of the most talented chefs in the industry.
